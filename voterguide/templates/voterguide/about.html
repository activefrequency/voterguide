{% trans "About NARAL Pro-Choice Massachusetts" %}

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

{% blocktrans %} Since 1972, NARAL Pro-Choice Massachusetts has led the way in advancing reproductive freedom for women and families in the Commonwealth. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Our mission is to develop and sustain a grassroots constituency that uses the political process to guarantee every woman the right to make personal decisions regarding the full range of reproductive choices, including preventing unintended pregnancy; bearing healthy children; and choosing safe, legal, accessible abortion. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} With the right to choose under attack across the country, we need more elected officials here who will stand up and safeguard a woman's ability to make personal, private decisions about pregnancy and childbearing. That's why, each campaign season, we work to elect pro-choice candidates by providing tactical support to their campaigns and mobilizing the pro-choice majority to vote. {% endblocktrans %}


{% trans "About Our Ratings" %}

{% blocktrans %} Ratings are based on a candidate's voting record, responses to our questionnaire, and/or information gathered by the NARAL Pro-Choice Massachusetts Political Committee. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Endorsed" %}

{% blocktrans %} Candidate is endorsed by NARAL Pro-Choice Massachusetts. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Pro-Choice" %}

{% blocktrans %} Candidate believes abortion should be safe, legal, and accessible; supports access to family planning services and comprehensive health education, including information about abstinence and contraception. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Anti-Choice" %}

{% blocktrans %} Candidate opposes a woman's right to choose abortion or supports it in such limited circumstances as to render the right to choose meaningless. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Mixed" %}

{% blocktrans %} Candidate supports a woman's right to choose with restrictions. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Unknown" %}

{% blocktrans %} Candidate's stance is unclear. {% endblocktrans %}


{% blocktrans %} Note: Some legislators rated "Anti-Choice" or "Mixed" support access to family planning services and/or comprehensive health education. {% endblocktrans %}


{% trans "Endorsement Policy" %}

{% blocktrans %} NARAL Pro-Choice Massachusetts endorses candidates with strong pro-choice positions and structured campaigns. Since we place a high priority on issue leadership and a pro-choice voting record, in certain races we have endorsed one pro-choice candidate over another. {% endblocktrans %}
