Since 1972, NARAL Pro-Choice Massachusetts has led the way in advancing reproductive freedom for women and families in the Commonwealth.

Our mission is to develop and sustain a grassroots constituency that uses the political process to guarantee every woman the right to make personal decisions regarding the full range of reproductive choices, including preventing unintended pregnancy; bearing healthy children; and choosing safe, legal, accessible abortion.

With the right to choose under attack across the country, we need more elected officials here who will stand up and safeguard a woman's ability to make personal, private decisions about pregnancy and childbearing. That's why, each campaign season, we work to elect pro-choice candidates by providing tactical support to their campaigns and mobilizing the pro-choice majority to vote.


About Our Ratings

Ratings are based on a candidate's voting record, responses to our questionnaire, and/or information gathered by the NARAL Pro-Choice Massachusetts Political Committee.

Endorsed

Candidate is endorsed by NARAL Pro-Choice Massachusetts.

Pro-Choice

Candidate believes abortion should be safe, legal, and accessible; supports access to family planning services and comprehensive health education, including information about abstinence and contraception.

Anti-Choice

Candidate opposes a woman's right to choose abortion or supports it in such limited circumstances as to render the right to choose meaningless.

Mixed

Candidate supports a woman's right to choose with restrictions.

Unknown

Candidate's stance is unclear.


Note: Some legislators rated "Anti-Choice" or "Mixed" support access to family planning services and/or comprehensive health education.


Endorsement Policy

NARAL Pro-Choice Massachusetts endorses candidates with strong pro-choice positions and structured campaigns. Since we place a high priority on issue leadership and a pro-choice voting record, in certain races we have endorsed one pro-choice candidate over another.
